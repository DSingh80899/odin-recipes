Pasta

Description

Tacos: a weeknight dinner staple! This flavorful taco recipe is made with seasoned ground beef taco meat and your favorite taco toppings, wrapped in warm tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 medium white onion, diced
  • 1 lb. ground beef, lean
  • 8 oz. tomato sauce
  • 3 tbsp homemade taco seasoning, or store bought
  • 8 medium flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup shredded taco cheese blend
  • 1 large roma tomato, diced
  • 4 tbsp sour cream
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Combine the onion and ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat and cook, until ground beef is cooked through and crumbled. Drain any excess fat and return skillet to stove top.
  2. Add in the tomato sauce and taco seasonings and stir over medium-low heat. Let the mixture simmer, until the sauce is thickened and flavors have had a chance to enhance, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Serve the taco meat while hot with the tortillas and other toppings of your choice. Enjoy!
